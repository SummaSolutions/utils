PayPal Payments Pro

If you don’t currently accept credit cards online or aren’t happy with your credit card processing service, PayPal Payments Pro is a flexible, customizable payment solution. Create the checkout experience that’s right for your customers – everything from look and feel to back-end integration.

  • Customers stay on your website
  • Simplified PCI compliance
  • Accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover and PayPal
  • U.S. customers can get financing with Bill Me Later
  • Automatic fraud screening included
  • Process phone, fax and web orders instantly with Virtual Terminal (an online version of a credit card swipe device)
  • Recurring billing (an optional add-on) automatically debits a customer’s credit card or PayPal account for subscriptions, membership dues or installment payments

By adding as a payment option, businesses can enjoy, on average, an 18% Sales increase and a 14% savings on credit cart processing cost

PayPal Express Checkout

PayPal Express Checkout is built for businesses that currently accept credit card payments online and are happy with their checkout experience. Customers pay on PayPal’s secure website, then return to your site to complete the transaction.

  • Lets buyers speed through checkout
  • Includes Bill Me Later®
  • Accepts international currencies
  • Compatible with top 3rd-party shopping carts
  • Automatic fraud screening at no extra charge
  • Optimized for mobile checkout

More than 80% of businesses noticed a bump in sales after adding paypal express checkout
